About

If I had to choose between awesome design and the world’s most delicious bacon, I would choose design. And I straight up LOVE bacon you guys.

I’m a tinkerer. Hands-on design warms me up inside. Which is good because I’m pretty small and a heat source like that will come in handy if I’m ever stranded in the Yukon with only my cunning and a Swiss Army Knife.

If we work together I may say the phrase “oh, man” a lot. Don’t be alarmed. I say that when I’m excited. It means I’m pumped about our idea. It definitely doesn’t mean that I just realized I left my oven on that morning. So no worries. I’ve got “turnning-off-ovens” on lock.

In all seriousness though, I’ve got a Masters of Science in Design from ASU and a Post-Graduate Certificate from the Creative Circus. I love design. And if you let me design things for your agency, then I love you too.
